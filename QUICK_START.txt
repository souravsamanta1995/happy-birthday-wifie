❓ COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: My photos don't show up. What's wrong?
A: Check filenames are EXACT and in /public/images/ folder

Q: Can I change the message?
A: Yes! Edit components/WishMessage.tsx

Q: Can I change colors?
A: Yes! Change Tailwind classes in component files

Q: Does it work on mobile?
A: Yes! Fully responsive and touch-friendly

Q: How do I deploy?
A: Click "Publish" in v0 or download and push to GitHub
